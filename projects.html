Zürich Disc Golf Open

The Zürich Disc Golf Open (ZDGO) is a 15 year running disc golf tournament that takes place yearly on the second weekend of July, organized by the Voodoo Warriors Disc Golf Club in Zürich, Switzerland. The last three years (2023, 2024 and 2025) I have been the Assistant Tournament Directory along side my good friend Vitus Meier as the Tournament Director. At the end of this years tournament the club asked me to step in as the Tournament Directory in 2026. I've accepted and I'm looking forward to organizing the tournament with the club as the Tournament Director next year!

We see ZDGO as a way to push the level of disc golf tournaments offered in Switzerland to the next level. We aim to organize a well-run and challenging tournament that provides a memorable weekend of disc golf to players from across Switzerland and Europe.
